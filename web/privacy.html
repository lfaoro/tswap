Troca

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2024

Overview

Troca is a command-line interface (CLI) application that prioritizes your privacy. We believe in transparency and minimal data collection.

Data Collection

Troca does not collect, store, or process any personal data. Our application:

  • Does not track user activity
  • Does not store personal information
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not maintain user accounts

Third-Party Services

Troca acts as a relay for cryptocurrency transactions to third-party services. Please note:

  • When using Troca to interact with third-party services, those services may collect data according to their own privacy policies
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services before use
  • We do not control and are not responsible for the data collection practices of these third-party services

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through our GitHub repository.
